CAESEN

🎮 Free educational games

Ten games for primary school, lower secondary (ESO) and newcomer classes, made in the evenings by a secondary school teacher in Catalonia. They all run right in the browser — computer, tablet, phone or interactive whiteboard — nothing to install, no sign-up, no ads. Most are in Catalan (they were built for Catalan schools); the English-learning ones are in English.

Every game has an info page with everything a teacher needs: what it teaches, which ages it suits and which game modes it includes. The demos are free forever; if a game saves you an afternoon of work, you can buy me a coffee.
